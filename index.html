The Problem with Saving Things

We save articles, threads, and papers with good intentions. They pile up — unsearched, unread, disconnected. Two years ago I had 3,000 bookmarks I couldn't recall or query. The more I saved, the less I could find. I built Research Vault to turn that growing pile into a queryable knowledge graph.

What I Built

A personal intelligence system that ingests saves, enriches them with Claude, and synthesizes them into topic articles, connection notes, and daily briefings. The vault grows more useful the more I use it — each save makes the whole graph smarter.
